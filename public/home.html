At Cpr Plus of Arizona, we are passionately committed to spreading awareness about the significance of CPR and first aid, and making a positive impact on the communities we serve. We take pride in delivering the most up-to-date and comprehensive training courses that can make a real difference in emergency situations. Our services include community CPR training, healthcare provider BLS certifications, first aid, pet first aid and CPR, as well as bloodborne pathogen education courses. We have also partnered with the Arizona Motorcycle Safety Awareness Foundation to offer their Accident Scene Management Course.

We believe that education is the key to ensuring the safety and well-being of individuals, families, and communities. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to providing engaging and effective training programs that equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to respond to a range of emergencies.
We are honored to serve the residents of Arizona and beyond, and are committed to upholding the highest standards of excellence in all that we do. Thank you for considering Cpr Plus of Arizona for your training needs.
