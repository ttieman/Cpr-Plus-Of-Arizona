Accident Scene Management

Discover the essential skills to handle accident scenes with our Accident Scene Management class. This course will equip you with the knowledge to safely manage an accident scene, provide basic first aid to injured riders, and effectively communicate with emergency medical services. Topics covered include emergency scene assessment, first aid, helmet removal, and more. Ideal for motorcyclists and other road users, this class will empower you to take action in critical situations. Complete the course to receive your AMSAF Accident Scene Management certification.
