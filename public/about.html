About Us

Our Story

Welcome to CPR Plus of Arizona, an EMT-owned and operated CPR training provider founded by Mark Laclair, a licensed EMT in Arizona and a retired EMT Intermediate and Level 2 Firefighter with over 14 years of experience in Keene, New Hampshire. Established in 2006, we initially served the community of Keene before migrating to Arizona in 2015 to expand our reach and continue our mission of bringing life-saving CPR training to communities across the state.

Our passionate and dedicated team strives to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to save lives in emergency situations. We believe that everyone should have access to quality CPR training, and our flexible approach ensures that we cater to the unique needs of our community. Join us today and become a part of our story as we create a safer, better-prepared world together.

Join us today and take the first step towards creating a safer environment for yourself and those around you. Together, we can make a difference and save lives.
